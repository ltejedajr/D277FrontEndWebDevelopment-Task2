Experience the breathtaking beauty of the New York City skyline adorned with iconic landmarks such as the majestic Brooklyn Bridge, the soaring Freedom Tower, and a mesmerizing array of other towering skyscrapers. Visit the Garden State today!

Art & Culture

From the luminous stages of Broadway to the winding corridors of the Guggenheim, New York State boasts unparalleled museums, galleries, theaters, and historic landmarks, offering a wealth of opportunities to immerse oneself in art and culture across the region. Unleash your inner creativity at esteemed institutions like the Fenimore Art Museum and the Rochester Contemporary Art Center, renowned for attracting visionaries from around the world. Dive into laughter in Jamestown at the National Comedy Center or celebrate the comedic legacy of Lucille Ball at the Lucy Desi Center for Comedy, featuring captivating exhibits and festivals that draw top comedic talents. Explore interactive exhibits and engaging activities at museums such as The Strong Museum of Play and the Corning Museum of Glass.

Whether your passion lies in Shakespearean drama, opera, ballet, jazz, musical theater, or witnessing world-class musical performances, New York's iconic venues—from Lincoln Center and Carnegie Hall to Shea's in Buffalo and the Saratoga Performing Arts Center—promise unforgettable experiences for all aficionados of the performing arts.

Empire State

Welcome to New York State, where vibrant cities, picturesque landscapes, and a rich tapestry of culture await you at every turn. From the towering skyscrapers of Manhattan to the tranquil shores of the Finger Lakes, there's something here to captivate every traveler's heart. Immerse yourself in the electrifying energy of New York City, where the bright lights of Broadway dazzle theatergoers, and museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Museum of Modern Art beckon art enthusiasts from around the globe. Explore the iconic neighborhoods known for street art scene where the rhythms of jazz and soul infuse the air with soul-stirring melodies. Venture beyond the city limits and discover the natural wonders that abound throughout the state. Hike through the majestic Adirondack Mountains, kayak along the scenic Hudson River, or marvel at the breathtaking beauty of Niagara Falls. For wine lovers, the vineyards of the Finger Lakes and the North Fork offer the perfect opportunity to savor award-winning vintages amid picturesque countryside vistas.
